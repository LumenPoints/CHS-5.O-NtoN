Gems of Zion · Day 5 Afternoon Detail

Three stops on the east side of the park. After a 4-hour canyoneering morning, the group will be tired — consider this a menu, not a checklist. Recommend Canyon Overlook + Hidden Swimming Hole, skip Petroglyph unless someone is energized.

Stop 1 · Anasazi Petroglyph Canyon

Location: ~2.5 miles past the east park entrance — small wooden fence on left

Park at fence · drop into creek bed · walk under road through small tunnel

Within 100 yds, take small trail up to a rock shelf · go left then right looking for petroglyphs

Don&apos;t stop until you find the bighorn sheep

Effort: Low · Time: 30–45 min

Stop 2 · Canyon Overlook Trail · ★ Recommended

Location: 2.5 mi further toward Springdale · park near the large tunnel (east side)

Take the steps up from the mouth of the tunnel

Distance: 2 miles round trip

Payoff: One of the best views in the entire park

Effort: Moderate · Time: 60–90 min

Stop 3 · Hidden Swimming Hole · ★ Recommended

Note: Locally nicknamed &quot;Nudie Pools&quot; — the name is folklore, in daytime with a group it&apos;s a swim spot

Location: Through the large tunnel, second switchback after — west-facing turn, pull-out on the left

Cross the road · find trail by rock wall · down into creek bed

Walk upstream ≤300 yds toward the arch · swimming pool with jumping rock

Bring: Swimsuits and a towel · this is the payoff stop
